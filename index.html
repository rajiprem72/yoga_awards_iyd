About the Awards

The Yoga Awards is a prestigious annual recognition honoring outstanding contributions in Yoga, Siddha, Ayurveda, holistic wellness and traditional healing systems. It celebrates excellence, dedication and service by individuals and institutions uplifting society through health, consciousness and holistic living.

Held in connection with International Yoga Day.

Terms & Conditions

  1. Open to individuals, institutions, yoga studios and wellness professionals.
  2. Self and third-party nominations accepted.
  3. No nomination fee.
  4. Last date: 10 June 2026.
  5. Documents may be requested for verification.
  6. Jury decision is final.
  7. False/incomplete info may disqualify.
  8. Previous winners cannot apply for same category again.
  9. Organizers may accept/reject/withhold awards.

Eligibility

  • Life Time Achievement – 20+ years contribution.
  • Yoga Guru – 5+ years.
  • Yoga Studio – 3+ years active.
  • Bala Yogi – State/National/International proof.
  • Siddha – 10+ years.
  • Ayurveda/Homeopathy/Unani – 3+ years.

Selection Criteria

  • Service & experience
  • Achievements & impact
  • Authenticity & ethics
  • Contribution to society
  • Promotion of traditional wisdom
